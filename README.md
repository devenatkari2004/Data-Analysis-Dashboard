# Vrinda Store Data Analysis (Interactive Excel Dashboard)
## Project Objective
To analyze the store’s 2022 sales data and create a clear dashboard that helps the owner understand customers better and improve sales in the next years.

## Process
I checked the data for missing values and errors, corrected any inconsistencies, and ensured the dataset was clean and properly formatted. After this, I built pivot tables as per the analysis requirements and integrated them into one interactive dashboard, enhancing it further with slicers for dynamic filtering.

## Project Insights
-Women customers purchase significantly more products than men, contributing to around 65% of total sales.

-Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top three states with the highest number of buyers.

-The adult age group (30–49 years) contributes the most to sales, accounting for nearly 50% of total purchases.

-Most customer orders come from major online platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.

-Over 90% of the products purchased were successfully delivered, indicating strong operational performance.

## Conclusion
If Vrinda Store wants to scale in today’s digital-first world, the smartest move is to tap into its strongest audience—women aged 30–49 from Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh. This group is highly active online and responsive to lifestyle-focused content. With strategic Instagram/Facebook ads, eye-catching reels, influencer partnerships, festive promotions, and personalized offers, Vrinda Store can amplify brand visibility, boost conversions, and create long-term loyal customers.
